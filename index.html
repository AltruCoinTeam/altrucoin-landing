About

AltruCoin is a decentralized cryptocurrency on BNB Smart Chain. Every transaction funds charitable initiatives in real-time through automated, transparent donations. Built for impact.

How It Works

  • 3% to verified charities (in USDT)

  • DAO voting to choose causes
